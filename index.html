Australia's Gambling Addiction
Australia is the world's biggest loser in gambling losses. In 2017, it ranked first in the world for the amount of money lost through gambling (Statista, 2017).

One of the most important indicator of tracking how prominent gambling is by analysing a demographics' gambling expenditure per capita.

Gambling expenditure refers to the net loss of a gambler or net profit for the house over a fiscal year.
